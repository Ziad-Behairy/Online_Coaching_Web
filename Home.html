What we have done

My name is Mahmoud Abdelhalim. a current full-time online coach, and a former personal trainer at Gold's Gym Egypt and many more with 4+ years of experience in the field. I started my online coaching business one year ago with the main goal of helping people change their bodies while managing all their life stresses and responsibilities. As a former PT, I used to sense people's frustration with the expensive personal training packages and lack of help from their coaches. Also, many people felt uncomfortable with their coaches due to poor communication and support. Moreover, people didn't feel like they're following a fitness program that fits their busy schedules and routines. So, I decided to fill in this gap in the market and give people exactly what they need and deserve!
